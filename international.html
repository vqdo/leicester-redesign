Study Abroad and Year in Industry

Where students incorporate a study abroad year in Europe or elsewhere or undertake a year in industry as an integral part of their undergraduate degree programme, the fees for that year are:

Erasmus exchange 25% of applicable fees for the year
Study abroad, non-Erasmus 25% of applicable fees for the year
Year in industry 25% of applicable fees for the year

Where students study abroad or undertake an industrial placement of one semester, the applicable tuition fees are the standard fees for the year of study.
